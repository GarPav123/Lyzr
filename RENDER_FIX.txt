URGENT: Set Environment Variable in Render Dashboard

The frontend at https://lyzr-1.onrender.com cannot connect to the backend.

ACTION REQUIRED:
1. Go to https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click your frontend service "lyzr-1"
3. Go to "Environment" tab
4. Add environment variable:
   Key: NEXT_PUBLIC_API_URL
   Value: https://lyzr-c2rq.onrender.com
5. Save - it will auto-redeploy

After this, your app will work!

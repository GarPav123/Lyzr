CRITICAL: Set Environment Variable in Render Dashboard

1. Go to your frontend service: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click on your frontend service
3. Go to "Environment" tab in the left sidebar
4. Click "Add Environment Variable" button
5. Enter:
   Key: NEXT_PUBeconomYBLIC_API_URL
   Value: https://lyzr-c2rq.onrender.com
6. Click "Save Changes"
7. The service will automatically redeploy

WITHOUT THIS VARIABLE, THE FRONTEND CANNOT CONNECT TO THE BACKEND!
